You have been invited to take part in a research study named "Joke Telling Robots"

Purpose of Research: This research study is being conducted to collect data on two different types of robot interactions.
What you will do in this study: If you decide to participate, you will first be asked to complete a brief demographic questionnaire, after which you will be presented with a video of a human-robot interaction scenario. You will then be asked to answer questions regarding the scenario. In order to progress through the study and receive your compensation code, you will be required to answer every question unless stated otherwise.
Time required: The session will take approximately 5 minutes to complete.
Risks and discomfort: There are no major foreseeable risks with this study.
Benefits: There are no direct personal benefits.
Compensation: You will receive $1.00 for completion at the end of the study.
Confidentiality: Your responses will be recorded but linked neither to personally identifiable nor worker information. The information will be kept strictly confidential and used only for research purposes. The documentation will be identifiable only by a randomly assigned ID number. All information will be kept on a secure web server.
Participation and Withdrawal: Your participation is completely voluntary. Should you decide at any time you no longer wish to participate you may discontinue your participation without penalty.
